What CognitivePrint detects
01 · NLP
Emotion classification

A fine-tuned RoBERTa model classifies each entry into 7 emotions with confidence scores.

02 · Graph theory
Cognitive drift detection

NetworkX builds a time-series graph of your entries. BFS traversal detects when distortion patterns cluster.

03 · Scoring
Health index

A composite 0–100 score weighing distortion frequency, sentiment, and trajectory over your last 7 entries.

04 · Pattern
Catastrophizing

Detecting language like "always", "never", "worst", "ruined" which are signs of worst-case thinking.

05 · Pattern
Black & white thinking

All-or-nothing language: "everything", "nothing", "everyone", "failure", "perfect".

06 · Pattern
Mind reading & self-blame

Assuming others' thoughts or taking responsibility for things outside your control.

How it works
①
Write a journal entry

Just a sentence or two about how you're feeling. No structure required, so just write naturally.

②
NLP pipeline analyzes your text

spaCy extracts linguistic features. A fine-tuned RoBERTa model classifies your emotion with confidence scoring. A rule-based distortion detector flags cognitive patterns.

③
Graph engine builds your pattern map

Each entry becomes a node. NetworkX connects them over time. BFS traversal detects drift, which is when your patterns worsen across multiple entries.

④
Your dashboard updates in real time

A force-directed D3.js graph visualizes your cognitive state over time. Your health index updates. Early warning flags surface if patterns worsen.
